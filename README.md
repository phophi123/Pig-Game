#Pig Game — How to Play 🎲

The game is for 2 players.
Players take turns rolling one die.
On your turn, you build a turn score:

Roll 2–6 → add it to your turn score.
Roll 1 → your current score resets to 0 and your turn ends.

At any time, you can hold to add your current score to your total score and pass the turn to the next player.
Play continues until a player reaches 50 points. That player wins!

Goodluck!
